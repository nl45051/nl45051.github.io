Nemo Lee is an asian-american born on August, 26, 1998 in Spartanburg, South Carolina. He is part of the rare hmong people who immigrated from China, Laos, Thailand, and Vietnam after the Vietnam war. He lived in Boiling Springs, South Carolina for 13 years. After his father recieved a job from Blackbaud, Inc., they moved to Charleston, South Carolina. They lived there for 2 years and while staying as a resident, Nemo attended River Oaks Middle School. Soon after middle school, Nemo's mother found a new job in Alpharetta, Georgia with Cellairis, Inc., a phonecase making company. Once his father received permission to work remotely, Nemo's whole family moved to Georgia to start a new life. Nemo finished his high school career at Duluth High School and now attends the University of Georgia as a Management Information Systems major. He is expected to graduate in December 2020.
